For schools who have been using DI for a year or more, we offer paid professional development services via Zoom or on-site. Often a refresher training is needed to ensure that delivery is optimum and that students are at mastery. Training is delivered by Claire Scott M.Ed., M. Psych – DI Teacher Trainer, Coach and Implementation Consultant.

Topics covered by PD

  • Interpreting data
  • Keeping documentation
  • Analysis of results and mastery tests
  • Help with delivery, correction procedures, pacing, signalling
  • Diagnosing issues in student results

This list is not exhaustive, and trainings can be tailored to suit your needs. ½ or full day PD sessions are available every Friday commencing Friday 7th March.

Join Our Weekly DI Drop-in Clinic!

Got questions about your McGraw Hill Direct Instruction programs? We’ve got answers! Our weekly Zoom drop-in clinics are the perfect opportunity to connect with your dedicated McGraw Hill local education consultant and get expert advice.

From troubleshooting results to managing student absences, and planning your next steps after Spelling Mastery, no question is too small or too big. Plus, it’s a chance to:

  • Chat with other DI teachers and practitioners.
  • Hear firsthand tips from Ambassador School teachers in your state who have successfully implemented DI in their classrooms.
  • Drop-in clinics are held weekly on Monday afternoons at 4pm.
  • Commencement Date: Monday 10th February (to end of Term)
